Capital Management
The Group’s objective when managing capital is to safeguard its ability to continue as a going concern and to
maintain an optimal capital structure so as to maximize shareholder value. In order to maintain or achieve an
optimal capital structure, the Group may adjust the amount of dividend payment, return capital to shareholders,
issue new shares or buy back issued shares. As at 31st March 2024, the Holding Company has only one class of
equity shares and has no debt. Consequent to the above capital structure, there are no externally imposed capital
requirements.
The Holding Company is having borrowings amounting to Nil (31st March 2023 Nil).
